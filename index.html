Why use Plottr?

By writers, for writers

Author C. Louis S. and Poet Steve Shepherd are the genius behind Plottr. We know what it's like to try to plot out stories without good tools.

Beautiful

We've all used ugly, hard-to-use writing software and it just gets in the way of your awesome story. Plottr is elegant and beautiful and easy to use. You'll have the next great American novel plotted out in no time.

You keep your work

Your work stays on your computer. We don't transfer it, we don't touch it. Ain't nobody gonna steal your ideas.
